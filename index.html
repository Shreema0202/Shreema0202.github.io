Myths:

Myths have been ruling the world for thousands of years. Over the years they may have changed and evolved, but at their core, they have stayed the same. In this context, at the end of the day, myths are still random opinions on random stuff, made up by random people.


Truth:

In society today, the truth is more often than not, considered as something to be unbelievable, implausible, rejected and ignored. Why is this, you ask? Well, it's because nowadays the truth is something way different to what we have made up in our minds and we prefer to live in ideal fantasies.
